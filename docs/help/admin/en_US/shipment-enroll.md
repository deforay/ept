# Enroll Participants

You are on this page because you clicked **Enroll** on a shipment.
Here you choose **which participants** should receive this
shipment.

The page has two sections side by side:

- **Left** — participants **not** on this shipment yet.
- **Right** — participants who **are** selected for this shipment.

Click any participant name to move them from one side to the
other. Nothing is saved until you click **Enroll** at the bottom.

## The quick way: auto-select tools

At the top of the page there are two tools that fill in the
selection for you, so you don't have to click names one by one.
Pick from either (or both) and then click **Apply to selection**.

### Preset Participant List

A preset list is a named, saved set of participants you (or a
colleague) created earlier. Choosing one and clicking **Apply**
**replaces** the current selection with that list.

### Copy from an Existing Shipment

This pulls in the participants who were enrolled in another
shipment, so you can reuse the same group without rebuilding it.

- Shipments for **this scheme** appear at the top of the list,
  newest first; shipments from **other schemes** are grouped
  below.
- Each entry shows the shipment code, its date, and how many
  participants it had.
- Applying a shipment **adds** its participants to the current
  selection — it does not wipe out what you already have. This
  means you can combine it with a preset list or with names you
  picked by hand.

After choosing a preset list and/or a shipment, click
**Apply to selection**. The matching participants move to the
right-hand (selected) side.

## Narrowing the list with filters

Click **Show advanced filters** to filter the available
participants by institute, country, region, network, site type,
and more. This is helpful when you have many participants and want
to enroll only a subset (for example, all labs in one province).

Click **Filter** to apply, or **Reset** to clear the filters.

## Reading the colours

Once you start moving people around, each name is tagged so you
can see what will happen when you save:

- **Already on this shipment** — they were enrolled before and
  will stay enrolled.
- **Pending ship (save to apply)** — newly added; they will be
  enrolled when you save.
- **Pending skip (save to apply)** — removed; they will be taken
  off the shipment when you save.

Use **Select All** / **Deselect All** to move everyone at once,
and the search box above each list to filter by name or code.

## Saving the selection as a preset

At the bottom, you can choose **Yes** to save this exact selection
as a **preset participant list** and give it a name. Next time you
enroll a shipment, that list will appear in the **Preset
Participant List** dropdown so you can reuse it in one click.

## Saving your work

Click **Enroll** to save. The participants on the right-hand side
become the enrolled participants for this shipment.

## Tips

- Enrolling participants does **not** make the shipment visible to
  them. Participants only see a shipment after you click
  **Ship Now** on its PT Survey.
- You can come back and change the enrollment any time before you
  ship. After shipping, it is better not to remove participants —
  they may already have started entering results.
- Only **active** participants can be enrolled. Copying from
  another shipment brings in just its active participants.
