# Shipments

A **shipment** is one set of test samples that you send out to
participants as part of a PT Survey. One PT Survey can hold one
or more shipments.

A shipment includes:

- A **scheme** (the test type, like HIV Serology or VL).
- A list of **samples** with their expected results.
- The **participants** you want to enroll for this shipment.

You always add shipments under a PT Survey. So the steps are:

1. First create the PT Survey (date and code).
2. Then add one or more shipments to it.
3. Then enroll participants in each shipment.
4. Then click **Ship Now** on the PT Survey page.

## What is on this page

This page shows all the shipments in your system. You can:

- **Search** by shipment code, scheme, or PT Survey.
- **Sort** by clicking the column headers.
- Click **View / Edit** on any row to open that shipment.
- Use **Enroll** (red button) to add or remove participants for
  that shipment.

## Common things you do here

- **Add a new shipment** — go to **Manage → PT Survey**, find your
  survey, and click **Add Scheme** next to it. The new shipment
  will then appear on this page.
- **Add or remove participants** — find your shipment, click
  **Enroll**, move names between **Available** and **Enrolled**,
  then click **Enroll** to save.
- **Edit samples or the result due date** — click **View / Edit**
  on the shipment, change the fields, then save.

## When a shipment becomes visible to participants

Shipments are **not** visible to participants right after you
create them. Participants only see a shipment after you click
**Ship Now** on its PT Survey. Until then, you can keep changing
samples, enrollments, and the due date safely.

## Tips

- A PT Survey with **no shipments** cannot be shipped. The
  **Ship Now** button will not appear.
- Only participants who are already enrolled in the **scheme**
  (Configure → Scheme Enrollments) can be enrolled in a shipment
  of that scheme.
- After you ship, it is better not to change samples or
  enrollments — participants may have already started entering
  results.
