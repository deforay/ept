# Add a Shipment

You are on this page because you clicked **Add Scheme** next to a
PT Survey, or you saved a new PT Survey and chose **Add and
continue to Shipment**.

A **shipment** is one set of test samples for one scheme (for
example, one DTS HIV Serology panel). You are about to set up that
panel under the PT Survey you just created.

## Don't worry — this is the next step, not a new task

Many users get confused here because the page looks new. You did
not lose your PT Survey. The survey is saved. This page is just
**step 2**: tell the system what samples will be inside that
survey.

If you close this page without saving a shipment, your PT Survey
will still exist — but it will sit in the **Not yet fully
configured** state and you will need to come back and add a
shipment before you can ship it.

## What you need to fill in

### Scheme

Pick the test type from the dropdown — for example,
**Dried Tube Specimen — HIV Serology**.

Only schemes that are switched on in your system will appear here.

### Shipment Code

A short code that identifies this shipment.

- The system will fill in a code for you. You can change it if
  you want.
- Use letters, numbers, and hyphens only.

### Result Due Date

The last date by which participants must send in their results.

- Pick a future date.
- Participants will see this date on their portal.

### Samples

Click **+ Sample/Control** to add a sample row. For each sample:

- Give it a sample name or number (for example, `S1`).
- Pick the **expected result** from the dropdown (this is what
  the correct answer should be).
- Add as many samples as your panel needs — usually 3 to 5.

You can use the **Remove** button to delete a sample row you do
not want.

## What happens when you save

Click **Add Shipment** at the bottom.

- The shipment is saved under the PT Survey.
- You are taken to the **Enroll** page, where you choose which
  participants get this shipment.

After enrolling participants, go back to **Manage → PT Survey**
and click **Ship Now** to actually send the shipment to
participants.

## Tips

- Add **all your samples and expected results before saving** — it
  is much easier than coming back to edit later.
- You can add **more than one shipment** to the same PT Survey
  (for example, one HIV Serology panel and one VL panel on the
  same date). Just come back to **Manage → PT Survey** and click
  **Add Scheme** again.
